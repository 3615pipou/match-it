Instructions

This game can be played in multiple ways among two to multiple players depends on the deck size. Plyers have to find matching symbols in 2 cards.

In easy version, two exactly same symbols have to be matched. In difficult version, two symbols which belongs to the same group has to be matched. Eg. suppose there are 5 groups of animals: dog, cat, lizard, insects, and birds. Two cards may match same image or different images belong to the same group.

You can increase the complexity with similar looikng, same color, or same groups. Eg group of medical equipments and construction euipments.

Upload one image in each group for simple version or upload multiple images in each group to make it diffcult.
